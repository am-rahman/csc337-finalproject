Chatter is a microblogging social channel where you can post your thoughts and follow others! To start using Chatter, you will need to:

1) First create an account, after which you will be automatically redirected to the login page.

2) After you successfully log in with your account credentials, you will be brought to your feed where you can see chatter posts from all other users!

3) When logged in, the navigation sidebar to the left will direct you to view your feed, settings, search, and this help page.

4) In your feed, you will see posts from all Chatter users. Hit the thumbs up button if you see something you like!

5) You can also search for other users from the search page. Find people you know and show your appreciation for their chats! Happy chatting!
